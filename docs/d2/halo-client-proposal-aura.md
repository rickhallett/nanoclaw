## Your Personal AI System — What to Expect

### How It Works

We'll build you a dedicated AI assistant that lives in Telegram — your own private bot, tailored to your voice, your rhythm, and your work. It learns your preferences, remembers your context across conversations, and can be extended with tools specific to your practice.

This isn't a shared service. It's yours — your own system running on its own infrastructure, with your own data that nobody else can access.

### The Process

**Week 1 — Getting to Know You**

A series of relaxed conversations through a temporary Telegram bot. No forms, no questionnaires. We talk about your work, your daily rhythm, the tools you already use, what you'd want an assistant to handle, and — just as importantly — what it should never touch. I'll also sit down with you once or twice to go deeper on specific areas.

By the end of this, I'll have a detailed blueprint for your system.

**Week 2 — Building & Testing**

I take the blueprint and build your personalised instance. This includes your custom personality and voice, your preferred schedule for check-ins or summaries, and any specific tools you need (content support, client communication templates, practice tracking, etc.).

You'll get access to a test version to try before anything goes live.

**Week 3 — Go Live & Tune**

Your system goes live on dedicated infrastructure. The first week or two is a tuning period — we adjust the personality, refine how it responds, and make sure it fits naturally into your day.

### What's Included

- Dedicated Telegram bot with your custom AI personality
- Personalised system prompt shaped by our onboarding conversations
- Memory system that learns and retains context across sessions
- Weekly usage summary delivered through the bot
- Secure, isolated infrastructure (your data stays yours)
- 2 hours per month of ongoing adjustments included

### Support

I respond to issues within 24 hours on weekdays. If something breaks on a weekend, I'll pick it up Monday unless it's genuinely urgent. You message me directly — no ticket systems, no queues. The 4 included hours each month cover bug fixes, tweaks, and new features. I track them honestly and let you know where you stand.

### Your Data

Everything your system generates — memories, conversations, tracked metrics — belongs to you. If you ever want to leave, I'll export everything and hand it over. No lock-in, no questions asked.

No long-term commitment. If it's not working for you after the first month, you can walk away.
