SRE Book (Google, free online), Architecture of Open Source Applications (aosabook.org, free), Increment (Stripe), DORA State of DevOps reports, Cloudflare/GitHub/GitLab/Honeycomb engineering blogs. Pattern to look for: decisions that seemed right at the time and why they broke under unanticipated conditions. Borrowed scar tissue maps onto learning in the wild: consequences you did not personally suffer but can internalise through honest post-mortems.
