If your goal is to be a great anthropologist and you have outsourced much of the statistical calculation, where you truly become seasoned is not inside a textbook on research methods, but out there in the field, writing papers, publishing books, watching those books get torn apart, all of that, and everything in between. That felt sense or depth takes decades to develop, and is largely dependent on having the opportunity to learn it. A certain amount of fundamentals is necessary. How much is nuanced. The basics impulse was a wrong turn not because basics do not matter but because the wrong gap was diagnosed.
