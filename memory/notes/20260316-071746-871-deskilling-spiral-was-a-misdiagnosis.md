The gap was not lacking fundamentals but lacking confidence that fundamentals were sufficient. Different problem, different fix. Job descriptions are wish lists written by people who often do not know what they need. Using them as a mirror then presenting that distorted reflection to an LLM framed to confirm the fear is a pattern to recognise and interrupt.
