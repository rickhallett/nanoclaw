The more people involved in a decision, the more average it tends to get, unless extraordinary measures ensure decisiveness and clarity of vision. Solo founders avoid this by default. In organisations, it requires disagree-and-commit discipline. Relates to the discipline-beats-swarm finding from SD-326.
