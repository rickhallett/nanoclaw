The number of agents you manage matters less than how fast you can triage and make high-quality decisions. The constraint on output is decision quality per unit time, not breadth. This is an executive-level skill now required at the individual contributor level. Operationalised as: compressed status in, binary judgment calls out, agents execute until next check-in. Maps to muster format and reportctl briefing in halos.
