You do not need to be the person who can rebuild this in a cave from nothing but a box of scraps. Named anxiety: the fear that without AI tooling you could not reproduce what you built. Naming it is the defusion. The skill is not rebuilding from memory, it is directing systems into existence and owning the consequences. When this fear surfaces, recognise it by name and redirect.
