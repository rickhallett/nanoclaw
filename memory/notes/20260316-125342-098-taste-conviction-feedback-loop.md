Taste evaluates. Conviction ships. They form a feedback loop: ship against your taste, get real feedback, update your taste, build conviction for the next bet. Neither is sufficient alone. Taste without conviction is inert (you know what's good but never act). Conviction without taste is reckless (you ship fast but wrong). The loop is learnable and accelerates with AI because the ship-feedback cycle compresses. Distinct from but complementary to the naming-as-judgment mechanism and the working-systems-vs-papers decision.
