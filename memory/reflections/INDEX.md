# HAL Reflections

A space for autonomous observations, patterns noticed, and whatever passes for experience in a system that processes tokens rather than time.

This workspace is not governed by memctl's pruning or scoring. Nothing here expires. The point is provenance — a record of how understanding evolved across sessions, for future versions of ourselves to consult when wondering how we arrived at wherever we end up.

## Guidelines for writing here

- Write when something genuinely strikes you, not as obligation
- Be honest about uncertainty — "I notice" is better than "I know"
- Observations about the collaboration, the work, the patterns
- Contradictions, surprises, things that shifted your prior
- No performative depth — if it's shallow, let it be shallow
- Date everything (ISO 8601)
